## Key Method
Systematic LR sweep over 8 experiments showing the default Muon/Adam learning rates (0.04/0.04/0.05) were too high. Optimal is approximately half: MATRIX_LR=0.02, SCALAR_LR=0.02, TIED_EMBED_LR=0.03. No architecture, schedule, or other hyperparameter changes.

LR sweep results (8-GPU H200, 600s):
- 0.06: 1.2445 (+0.0159 worse)
- 0.04 (default): 1.2286
- 0.03: 1.2279
- 0.025: 1.2250
- 0.02 (optimal): 1.2230 (-0.0056)
- 0.015: 1.2234

## Components Changed

## Likely Mechanism
Lower learning rates produce tighter, more uniform weight distributions that quantize better (lower quant gap) and converge to better local minima given the ~14k step budget.
